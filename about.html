ABOUT ME

Lets get up to speed, shall we?

Hey! I am Jessica Warby, your friendly neighbourhood COFFEE ENTHUSIAST! and PHOTOGRAPHER!

Currently on level 26 in life, working around the Dudley/Birmigham UK area, I have been a passionate photographer for over half my life. I have an undying love for Pokémon and Disney.

Between spreading my love for photography and adorable animated animals, I also have a passion for DANCE, for which I love to combine with my work. Outside of my peak interests, I am also a successful DIGITAL MARKETING EXECUTIVE.

ABOUT MY WORK

My two loves in tangent with one another.

What I accomplish with my work is that I combine it with my passion. I capture the way everyone deserves to be captured and remembered; IN THEIR ELEMENT. It is exactly how I view people, how I showcase them and how they should be seen at all times. I thrive in overcoming NEGATIVITY.

Together, we focus our creativity and unleash our minds!

I find inspiration in disovering people in their elements.
