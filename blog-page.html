Thy love for Dance! NEW

I have been blessed with having amazing friends with amazing talents! Just before our lockdown, we all managed to get together and combine our talents and our passion into one. It was a small session that we had. Yet the bonds we create, the fun we had, and the pictures I managed to get from these two talented ladies, was definitely time well spent.

One of my lovely ladies, Sophie, currently in her final few months at Leicester University, close to becoming a wonderful nurse. She took a step back away from Dance to pursue her career and her education. Luckily though, she hasn't missed a beat, and we were able to grab these amazing shots of her in action!

In between those shots, we have Shannon! We met in our work-place whilst studying at University. Shannon has a Degree in Dance! So I was lucky to have met her. We both helped each other with our respective degrees,and ended up being successful! She is now a mother of two beautiful girls, so soon enough she shall be their dance instructor.

Once lockdown has ended here in the UK, you can be sure that all of us shall meet again and create more moments like these.

Adventures of the LEGO crew!

Behold! The LEGO Crew!

They have been best friends for years and years! LEGO lockdown has ended and they have been able to resume their lives! After spending a few months talking to each other through cameras, the Crew manage to finally meet up once again and plan a last-ditch getaway!

The plane lands, and the first thing the Crew do is get their rental car. They go off in search of a place to rest and have some food before the evening. They finally get into the pub. Getting in those beers, enjoying the moment. The Crew however, are having a little too much fun! It ends up with the Crew crashing their car!

What is going to happen to them? Stay Tuned for more Adventures!
